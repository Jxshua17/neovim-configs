## My Neovim setup

Well, this is my Neovim setup. Slowly building my configs from the ground up. 

I don't have a lot going on in my configs yet. The original reason for setting up neovim was because of my dissatisfaction with typst on vscode. It just looked ugly. And inspired by guys like [Evan Chen](https://web.evanchen.cc/) and his [video on Typst](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uqKNux8-pto) as well [Sylvan Franklin](https://www.youtube.com/@sylvanfranklin) who is a big preacher of the gospel of typst as his channel obviously shows when you take a look at it, I decided to ditch vscode and start from scratch with neovim by learning about things like parsers, language server protocols(LSPs), the various plugins you can have, keybindings and all of that shit(forgive my french).
